CTF player 🚩

Capture The Flag or CTF for short is a Security Hacking game made for hackers to exercise and develop their skills, the concept is by by finding the flag which is hidden in vunlerable system, the goal is to find this vunlerability and exploit it to had access to the system, there are 2 categories of CTF attack/defense (Competitors steal flags either from other competitors) && jeopardy (competitors from the organizers).For me, I don't played too much, I consider myself a normal player with basic skills.
